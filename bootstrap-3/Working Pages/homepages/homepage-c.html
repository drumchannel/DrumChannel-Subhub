Terry Bozzio

Drum Channel’s Artist-in-Residence, Terry is here to answer your questions and give you advanced lessons that will prepare you to become a Pro.

Chad Smith

Chad has helped produce numerous platinum recordings with The Red Hot Chili Peppers and has gained the recognition he has currently by producing one hit groove after another.

Neil Peart

Neil is the drummer and lyricist for the Canadian rock band Rush who have played together for more than 30 years and sold over 24.5 million records in the United States.

Peter Erskine

With one of the most versatile careers in drumming history both live and in the studio, Peter shares invaluable information to improve your playing.

John JR Robinson

First-call drummer for Quincy Jones and David Foster, JR’s career is legendary. In addition to teaching, JR is also a great host in DC Shows.

Alex Acuña

Modern Drummer readers acknowledge his talent as a drumset player and percussionist. Alex is a master of Afro-Cuban Fusion Jazz.

Chad Wackerman

One of the most versatile drummers of all time, Chad is at home in the studio on tour and at his home when he finds time to give lessons.

Danny Seraphine

Danny is the original drummer and founding member of the rock group Chicago. He created a style of drumming combining jazz and rock that has influenced generations of drummers.

Marco Minnemann

Marco is an author and has written three books including the award winning "Extreme Independence". He has also toured with death metalists Necrophagist and is a master of independence.

Rich Redmond

Rich is a producer, songwriter and first call Nashville drummer. In addition to being in Jason Aldean's band he has played with Bryan Adams, Kelly Clarkson, Jewel and many others.

Stewart Copeland

Chosen by the readers of Modern Drummer magazine as one of the "50 Greatest Drummers Of All Time” and best known for his precise, energetic, signature drumming with The Police.

Gregg Bissonette

One of the most in-demand drum teachers in the world, Gregg has done hundreds of recording sessions and movie soundtracks, along with touring and starring in Ringo's All-Starr Band.

Ralph Humphrey

Along with DC instructor Joe Porcaro, Ralph teaches at LAMA and together they have written the DC Academy online lesson curriculum. Ralph is also the drummer on Dancing With The Stars.

Joe Porcaro

Joe has made albums with artists such as Frank Sinatra and Madonna and L.A. Philharmonic Orchestra. He is one of the most highly respected drum and percussion teachers in the world.

Curt Bisquera

Curt is a first call session and touring drummer who has credits ranging from Mick Jagger, Sir Elton John, Tom Petty, Morris Day and the Time, and many others.

Mike Clark

Mike Clark's work with the Headhunters is legendary and he is one of the most sampled drummers in history. A master of Jazz and Funk.

Three great ways to learn

Different styles of lessons that cater to your needs and your prefered learning method. You can dive in and start learning right away with Secrets From the Pros or jump into a Master Class and get more in-depth.

Lessons

Longer, more in-depth lessons on a specific topic or idea. Ranging from beginner to expert, lessons have something for every level of player. (Typically 20-60 mins)

Secrets From the Pros

Short on time? Secrets from the pros are short, “micro” lessons designed for maximum impact and information in as little amount of time as possible. (Typically 2-10 minutes)

Master Classes

Looking to dive deeper? Masterclasses are a series of 5-15 min lessons on one specific topic or concept bundled together for a totally immersive experience. (Released periodically.)
