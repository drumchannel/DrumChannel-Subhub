Berklee Drum Set Audition - Course Preview

John Ramsay is the department chair for the Percussion Department of Berklee and he will walk you through your audition. John describes his duties as Chair of the percussion department at Berklee College of Music and the audition requirements. He covers Reading, Rudiments, Song Form, Time Feel and your Prepared Piece.
